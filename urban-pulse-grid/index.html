Urban Pulse — Grid Prototype
Click a square → it subdivides and zooms in

How it works

  1. Squares pulse to indicate they’re interactive.
  2. Click a square to zoom into it — it creates a finer grid inside.
  3. Use Back to go up one level; Reset to the full view.
